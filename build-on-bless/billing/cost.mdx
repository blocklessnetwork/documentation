## Testnet Phase (Beta)

During the testnet phase, all deployments on the Bless Network are free of charge. This allows developers to experiment, build, and deploy applications without any cost while the network is being optimized for scalability and performance.

<Warning>
    {' '}
    Bless Network is currently in **beta (Testnet)** and is **not yet ready for production
    use**. Pricing and billing models will be introduced in future mainnet releases.{' '}
</Warning>
