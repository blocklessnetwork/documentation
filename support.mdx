## Support and Feedback

<Note>
    For assistance with deploying applications on the Bless Network, visit the
    [Support page](/build-on-bless/support) in the Build on Bless section.
</Note>

If you have any questions or feedback, please reach out to us via our [Discord (Support Channel)](https://discord.com/invite/blessnetwork). We normally respond within 48 hours. We would love to hear your thoughts!

## Contributing

If you want to contribute to Bless, please check out our [GitHub repositories](https://github.com/blessnetwork). We welcome contributions of all kinds, including bug fixes, feature requests, and documentation improvements.

## Thank You

We'd like to thank everyone who has supported, provided feedback, and contributed to Bless. Your support is greatly appreciated.
